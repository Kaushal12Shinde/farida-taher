Meet the Chef

Hi! I am Farida and I am the founder of Wild Sugar Gourmet Baking in Mysore. I was a GMAT/GRE trainer at The Princeton Review before I took up baking!

I am professionally trained @whitecapsind International school of Pastry where I took up the Paris Patisserie program and a few courses @lavonneacademyindia. Getting a certification and learning professionally worked beautifully for me since it brought my vision to life. Wild Sugar was launched on September 3rd, 2020.

Today I own a baking studio and have launched 40+ online workshops with a 5000+ student base. Welcome to my page!
